World Class Instructors

Jon calestine, the former EBI world champion, is the head Jiu-Jitsu instructor of the grappling club.

Michael Donato--former olympian-- runs the Greco-Roman Wrestling Program at the Grappling Club.

Kevin Ramkishun--former High School Wrestling Coach -- runs the Freestyle Wrestling Program.

Top Quality Facility

The Grappling Club has top of the line training mats, lifting equipment and even showers. Jon and his team makes sure to clean the mats after every session. This ensures that they provide a clean environment for everyone to train in.

Training Environment

At the Grappling Club, Jon believes in a laid back training environment. Everyone trains hard and beats each other up but we treat each other like family. We share laughs, joke around and even help each other outside of the gym.
